Emotion Match

How to Play:

Objective: Match pairs of emotion cards as fast as you can.
Setup:
  • You can use the Arrow key to navigate the game
  • Upon starting the game, a grid of emotion cards will be displayed face-down.
  • Click on any two cards to reveal their emotions. If the emotions match, the cards will remain face-up. If not, they will be flipped face-down again.
Gameplay:
  • Use your memory to remember the locations of the different emotions as you uncover them.
  • Click on two cards consecutively to reveal their emotions and attempt to find matching pairs.
  • The game ends when all pairs have been successfully matched or the time runs out.
